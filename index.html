Covid-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.

It is a dangerous disease and needs care.

We are able to survive the pandamic with the help of social workers like doctors, cleaners ,etc.

The ways to prevent Covid-19 are:
1.Not going out often
2.Wearing mask
3.Following Social distancing
4.Washing hands regularly
